Home page

To sign in:

  • For Admin:
    • Click on 'admin' on the navigation bar.
    • Sign into the using the provided admin credentials.
  • For Students:
    • If you have an account, click login under student portal and login using your credentials.
    • If you do not have an account click 'sign up', and fill in the form.
  • For Recruiters:
    • If you have an account, click login under recruiter portal and login using your credentials.
    • If you do not have an account click 'sign up', and fill in the form.
    • For recruiter token, it must be provided by the admin
    • Recruiter Id is up to your choosing, it must be unique
    • Fill in the rest of the fields appropirately, such as username and password.

To sign out: click the logout button at the top left of the navigation bar.

Student dashboard

  • After signing in, use the form provided to add in your details.
  • To change any details, fill in the form again and click submit.
  • At the right of the page any interviews you have will be displayed.
    • Click the name of the recruiter and internship to see their details.
  • Your account can be deleted by clicking the 'delete account' button.
    To return to the student dashboard from the homepage, click the login button or sign-up button in the student portal card.

Recruiter dashboard

  • After signing in, you can use the form provided under 'add opportunities' to add new internships.
  • To change any details, fill in the form again and click submit.
  • At the right of the page any interviews you have will be displayed.
    • You can use the dropdown menu to change the outcome of the interview.
    • Click the name of the student to see the student details.
  • Your account can be deleted by clicking the 'delete account' button. This will delete any internships associated with your account.
    To return to the recruiter dashboard from the homepage, click the login button or sign-up button in the recruiter portal card.

Admin dashboard

Internship, student and recruiter search

  • These pages are used to search the database for either internships, students, or recruiters.
  • Use either the dropdown menus or the search fields to search the database.
  • Click either the Download Recruiters CSV, Download Internships CSV or Download Students CSV to download all of the recruiters, students, or internships.
  • Leaving the search fields blank will return all of the recruiters, students, or internships.

Compute matches

  • This page is used to see a list of computed matches and approve/disapprove them.
  • Click 'run algorithm' to run the algorithm and compute matches.
    • This will display a list of matches.
    • Use the approve/disapprove buttons to reject or accept matches.
    • Click on the name of the student or internship to see their details.

Company management tool

  • This page is used to register/delete companies and manage their details.
  • Under 'All companies' it displays a list of all companies.
    • Use the dropdown to filter the companies by ones that have or don't have a matched internship.
    • Click on the name of the recruiter to see their details.
    • Click the delete button to delete the company.
  • To register a new company, fill in the form on the left and click submit.

Analytics dashboard

  • This page is used to display various analytics about the data in the database.
  • Click 'new analysis' to display the charts again if the database has been modified.

Admin panel

The admin panel is used to see the database and make changes to it:

  • Navigate to the /admin page and login using the admin credentials.
  • There will be a list of tables on the left of the screen.
  • From these, you can view all the objects in that table and add, delete and edit them
